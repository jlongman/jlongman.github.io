N-Gage: More details

A couple of things about the N-Gage:


  • It doesn't work with Rogers GPRS

  • It's a Symbian Series 60 version 1.2 implementation, not version 2.0

  • It supports only MIDP 1.0, not MIDP 2.0

Obviously having the more up-to-date stuff would be cool, more power, etc. Still it's pretty neat, especially since I put Frozen Bubble on it. Now I need to find some cheap MMC cards...
