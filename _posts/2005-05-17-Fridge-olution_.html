Fridge-olution!

Awright, so grab any fridge, (energy efficient, bottom mount freezer, what have you), then paint it, wall paper/contact paper it, or replace the front of it.

This makes me happy, because it solves the aesthetic issues while allowing me to defer the cost of the looks until later. I'm sure Joan will want a 'Hello Kitty' refrigerator anyways.

Oh and get a fridge that doesn't need to be jammed shut by hand.
