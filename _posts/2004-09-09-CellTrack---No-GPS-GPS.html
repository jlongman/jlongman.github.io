CellTrack - No GPS-GPS

One of the cool things taking the train through Switzerland was that your cell phone would tell you the name of the city, town or village that you were in. I thought I would have to get a cell phone with GPS in order to get the same functionality, but it looks like the tool to do it is already here, it just needs some more software.

Thus, I have to think this is one of the coolest programs out there. (At least this minute, ) CellTrack is a program that tracks and logs the cell ID's you walk through. What's missing is the correlation with your location, at least everywhere except Germany. It seems to me that a large portion of this database will write itself, thanks to the logging. So I'm going to think about what it would take to create a database.
