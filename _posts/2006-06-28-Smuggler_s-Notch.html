Smuggler's Notch

The great climbing caravan made it up to Smuggler's Notch, in Stowe, VT, last weekend. It was great! What a change to come away from a weekend feeling great about it. (I.e. the weather held together!). And what a great (and big!) group! Check out g's superpost.

I'm pretty happy about sending 'A Dirty Job', V7. Sure it was a one-move wonder, but what a fun move and what fun to find the heel scum. I love it when a problem seems impossible and slowly but slowly, you bend to its will and then it goes. And sometimes it feels like the difference is you can put 2 pounds of pressure on the underside of an overhang, and bam, the impossible becomes ticked.

Speaking of the impossible - 'The Impossible Problem', also V7, feels like it could go. Interestingly, on the video here, they hide the key beta! Oh well, it'll be fun when it goes too.

A shout out to Alanna for taking one for the team. No doubt the ankle-gods will be appeased for a while and I'm sure she'll bounce back. If not, I recommend she go the Steve Austin route.
