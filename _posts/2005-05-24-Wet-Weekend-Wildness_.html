Wet Weekend Wildness!

Graham and a group of climbers from Ottawa invited me up to Rumney, NH for a wee bit of climbing. Well, actually they invited me up for a lot of climbing, but 'wee' was all that was left given the 'fantastic' rain.

Still I did some leading in the 5.10 range, and although I didn't do spectacularly, at least I feel I accomplished what I wanted to (in the limited time even!) and it certainly motivates me to get back into it. Really nice people who are themselves strongly motivated and encouraging do that.

I think Mel put it best, although she was talking about x-country skiing, the people who are there choose to be there and they are all really happy!

To be honest, after the 12 hours of rain and almost getting gassed at 4am by the people who had to retreat into the car when their tent got soaked (poor girls!), I was happy to head home. But I would have been much happier bashing my head against some dry rock!
