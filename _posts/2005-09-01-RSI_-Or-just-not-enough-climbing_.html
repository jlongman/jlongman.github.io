RSI? Or just not enough climbing?

I basically haven't climbed all summer, except for a day or two in July, so I'm wondering if that has something to do with the discomfort I'm feeling in my forearms these days.

I've also been more active on a keyboard, both the happy hacking, and my vaio laptop at home. It doesn't help that the ergonomics at work are only alright.

Anyways, I've found two little utilities that are helpful and free.

  • As in beer: Oostime. A simple no-frills tiny program that stops all input on windows
  • As in beer, and speech: WorkRave. A GTK app ported to windows with lots of configurability, statistics, built-in exercise demos, and even supports distributed use. And I think it was written by programmers, as it has a postpone button. Really important so you can kick off a build before you lock yourself out. (Is it just me, or could it also be wor-krave?)

Of course, the next step is to see if starting climbing again helps.
