Linux and the M2NPV-VM

Quick note to record my annoyance.

The M2NPV-VM, with the latest firmware (1401), used with a linux kernel more recent than 2.6.18 and KVM_GUEST configured in the kernel will not work with USB. Some details here. Mythbuntu 9.10 is shipped with this configuration. Generally, avoid 1401, also, from what I read.

I'd actually discovered that all a couple of weeks ago, but was pretty laissez-faire about usb until I realized my lirc config had been written over and was possibly on another drive - and the easiest way to attach and recover it would be via USB. That was all prompted by the machine crashing after recording - which is why I also updated the firmware on my seagate drives about the same time.

(Aside, my Seagate 1TB drives shipped limited by a jumper to 150Mb/s, but my mobo is supposed to be capable of 300 Mb/s.)
