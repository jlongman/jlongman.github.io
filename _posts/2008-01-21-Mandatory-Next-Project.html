Mandatory Next Project

I swear that I'm not a fanboy - I've hacked my iPhone, and installed (a partially working) iPhone Dev Toolchain on my Macbook.

I picked up an AppleTV, with which I hope to install a linux, HD-decoding capable MythTV on it.  Delicious!

So far I've got the 1.0.9 mb_boot_tv bootstick running.  Fortunately I came across instructions pointing out that the compiled copy available online is garbage.  I also had a backup of my laptop drive from when Tiger was on it.

Ideally, the next step is to build the netboot image to run the Linux Myth frontend with appropriate hacks and driver updates.  I may eventually look at modifying the AppleTV drive, but only after I've confirmed the hardware can hack HD.  And once I know about AppleTV Take 2.

I'll link this entry up later...
