Building the (New) Year's iCalendar

I'm not really a 'new year's resolution' kind of person - it doesn't really make sense to store up improvements you can make to yourself for the new year. Why not start right away?

So I was scanning some TreeHugger references, checking out some of the cool prefab places and it made me think that I'd missed a bunch of home shows and bike shows and straw house events etc...

So I've started to put together an iCal file which collects everything. Eventually I'll get past the ssh IP filter on this machine and I'll try to stick it in a place where other people could share it.
