Dear Friend,

Been climbing?

I picked up a Metolius Behemoth pad (TOO BIG!) and hit Val David last (er: last, last) weekend. Ran into people from the gym in morreal, so it was like a session.

The bugs were crazy! Highlight, sent V5 'Té Ou?'. Got on Poph-tart V5 but was a bit psyched by the rock behind you, nearly sent Rhythmix V5 but it was just too hot. Lowlight, by the end of the day after everyone else left I was still climbing when I couldn't take it anymore and ran screaming from the woods.

And my pad is almost too big. I thought big would be good if I climbed solo and didn't run into anyone up there. But it barely fits in the back of my friend's coupe. Damn those impulse purchases!

  2. j2 06.06.06 / 11am

    'Té Ou?' is on the Francoise boulder roughly between 'Louise Classique' and 'Poph-Tart'. It's a reddish boulder with a V8 ('Té chaud Paul?') on the corner and a V10 ('Ôcouto') underneath the roof where you start 'Té Ou?'. 'T.O'. goes up the middle and exits rightish. 'Rhythmix' is maybe 15 feet from it.

    I didn't get [back] on 'Louise Classique' as I was alone by the time I made it over there. I was bit psyched out by the rock behind it.
