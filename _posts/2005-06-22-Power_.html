Power!

The above graph basically shows that we've reduced the power consumption by about 40% since December.

I attribute the dramatic difference to a couple of things:

  1. I sealed the skylight with plastic during the winter
  2. I installed an electronically-controlled thermostat on the sleeping side of the apartment
  3. The radiators (which I don't pay for) worked better this year
  4. Joan has done her part by reducing the use of the dryer

I'm really happy about this, and I'm looking forward to seeing what difference the more efficient refrigerator makes. (oh, it's conceivable there could be little change, or even an increase, in power consumption because the new fridge is larger than the old, but I doubt it).

And we don't have an AC, as my place would be nearly impossible to cool, but I think blocking the skylight with reflective radiant barrier will make a difference in comfort level this year also.

(I'm aware that my installation of the radiant barrier isn't ideal, but I still believe it represents a net gain over not having it - and I can't see that 'proper' installation would make much of a difference.)
