Knoppmyth R5.5 and Mythbuntu 8.04.1 and fail

Neither one of these distributions would come up clean, but at least Mythbuntu was giving me enough information to look for things online. Knoppmyth I was really at a loss!

FINALLY I figured out that lirc was modprobe'ing and using the device from the USB VFD display and IR receiver instead of the IR Blaster on my PVR-150. In addition in Mythbuntu, networking was coming up before mysql, and this also screwed things up.


sudo rmmod lirc_imon
sudo /etc/init.d/lirc restart
sudo /etc/init.d/mysql restart
sudo /etc/ini.d/mythtv-backend restart

Will do wonders, assuming you've otherwise setup lirc correctly.

So I need to make things permanent and figure out how to stop the tuner cards from swapping devices on boots, as well as stop crashing every now and then. I may go back to Knoppmyth just because I'm more familiar with where it keeps files.
