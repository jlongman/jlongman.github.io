More on Mo:Blog

What I would like to see from Mo:Blog is the timestamp associated with when I entered the entry, not when I uploaded it. Really, the timestamp of when I upload something is completely uninteresting to me. (Well, almost).

The idea is to have a gateway for the pics I generate from the palm. I haven't been able to keep up with my blog at all in the last while, and I have barely touched a computer outside of work since my laptop's hard drive has started to do its impression of a chipmunk on speed, so I'd like this to happen automagically so I can still appear alive.

Appearance is everything.
