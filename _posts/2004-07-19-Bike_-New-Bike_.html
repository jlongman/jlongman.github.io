Bike! New Bike!

I haven't bought a new bike in over 8 years, as I figure it. And if you think about a nice bike, it's been more like 13. So meet my new bike... A Norco Napoli, a 'cross-trekking' bike - a slightly modified road bike frame with a straight handle bar and mountain bike components on it. I was just going to go for a road bike, seeing as my primary goal is biking the 15.7 km to work (according to mapquest, and that's taking the Decarie (or 15) highway.) (And of course, Montreal is much more hilly than Ottawa is.) â€” ahem, but I wanted to have something that was more versatile in terms of gravel trails. Or what have you. Because the bike has mtb-style components on it, I can fit fatter tires on it to make it act more like a hybrid.

So, I bought it late Saturday, but still haven't taken it for more than the test-drive spin. It has clipless pedals, and I just bought the shoes to attach myself to the bike. Human cyborg indeed!
