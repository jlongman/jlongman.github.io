on password security

Hydro has a wonderful client webpage that allows you to do all kinds of great things like look at your consumption. Unfortunately, they require a password with a number, unlike any of my other utilities. I generally do use different passwords with my utilities but I mix usernames and passwords up a lot between them, so typically it takes a while to get the right username and password combination.

Except they lock the account for 24 hours after 3 tries. Which can be really frustrating, as chances are I won't hit the right combination in less than 3 tries.

And what are they protecting? The ability to see your consumption, the ability to submit your meter reading and the ability to pay your bill. Possibly, *possibly*, you can cancel your account.
