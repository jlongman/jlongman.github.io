gamercard

Part of the Xbox Live experience is the gamercard - a total of your score across games, as well as your reputation and various images. I stuck one underneath the gallery on the sidebar. I think I'm okay with letting people know what I'm playing.

Kind of a neat viral marketing trick, having something you can include in your blog or bbforum (well, gamercard.net does that) postings, too.

Burnout Revenge is the current prospect. I've had it about a week, and it's pretty good. I'd love to see it in HD, and they definitely have a problem with load times, except the 'Crash' games which are intelligently done. They have stupid mandatory score total screens which can't be interrupted. Dumb guys, way to waste my time.
