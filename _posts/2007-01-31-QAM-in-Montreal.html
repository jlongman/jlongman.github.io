QAM in Montreal

I spent a bit of time goofing around with the system in order to determine whether ther's anything worth picking up on QAM in Montreal.  That's the digital encoding system used by cable operators to send digital TV.  Anywho, basically in Montreal, from a quick scan you can get: Radio and VOD advertisement channels.  Disappointing, as even if some of the local non-HD channels were available it would be an improvement in recording quality by skipping an ADA conversion.

The good news tho is that I can probably attach a splitter and get dual OTA HD recepetion.  Which is kinda cool, but also means that I'd need more hard disk space!

Besides, the real concern right now is getting a consistent signal for the shows Joan really cares about.  She gets quite emotional when they don't come in.  (Although a little searching on Torrent could solve the problem.  But, then one wonders why you pay for Cable at all, then?)
