I want my CPU back

Apparently there's a bug related to Check Sizes in Finder that causes it to use ~20-30% CPU constantly.   In short the workaround is to close all Finder windows (option-command-W).  Credit goes to Joe Maller: the workaround is detailed here (and a google-cached version here, but maybe I'm having problems because I'm in Europe).
