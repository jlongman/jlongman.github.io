Dream houses

I want to build my own house some day. I want it to be as geek gizmo as I am, and I want it to environmentally break even, or heck, why not dream, even be a net positive.

  1. This guy is a bit of a geek, but he's done some interesting stuff. I'm happy I found his site though, as he uses earth-sheltering which is a concept which I'd read about, and then lost the reference to it. He's also definitely a geek, as he's used iButton's 1-wire temperature sensors to monitor the heat exchanger in his system. (I have an iButton somewhere - it actually has a Java VM on it.) (I was thinking a gumstix might be a fun low-power platform...)
  2. Glen Hunter has also done some interesting things, I especially like the Gaudi-esque ceiling ideas and the fact that he's disconnected from the power grid. Oh, and he's made his own LED lighting...

Charlie's right, life's only worth living if you're downtown or you're out in cottage country.
