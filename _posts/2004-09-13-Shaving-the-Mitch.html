Shaving the Mitch

Oh, the other thing I did this weekend (Sunday actually) was shave Mitch. He was pretty patient considering. For such a neurotic dog he was great.

(He likes to be hand fed, he likes to drink his water from my water glasses - fortunately I haven't taken a drink from a glass that he's had already, yet and as far as I know- the list goes on.)

I got his body, but not his head, and some of it looks hacked, as I was trying to take the volume off and then trim the bottom later to make it easier on him, but he looked like he'd had enough.
