Energy consumption tip

A warm blanket can do wonders for keeping the heat down. Personally I'd choose a goose down duvet - there's a place here in Montreal that will do a queen size super thick one for $300 with a ten year warranty. Unfortunately, Joan is allergic to everything, so Ikea had a king-size synthetic duvet that is very down-like and super warm. I think it retails for $120 before, I got it from the As-is section for $99 with tax. Don't worry, I took the tacks out. I mean, don't worry, we washed it.

Joan still has a problem with it being TOO warm. I have an extra sheet on and I find it nice and toasty.
