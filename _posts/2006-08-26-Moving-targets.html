Moving targets

After upgrading to Knoppmyth R5C7 we suddenly became aware of server load. A 'Reschedule', which basically happens any time you touch the schedules or complete a recording, would cause stuttering in the front end. Myth is a complicated enough system it could be any number of things.

One plan of attack though was to look at /proc/interrupts, and lo and behold â€“ the analog capture card and the digital mpeg encoder card were on the same interrupt. So I shuffled the cards a bit and bang, now one of the USB devices is on the same interrupt as the software encoder. Oh well. Hopefully it'll be better.

In the meantime I moved some videos and music off one drive onto the other, so I could reformat the other drive with more inodes. I had messaged the myth-devel list suggesting perhaps their SCM drive was full, as I was given a disk full warning when checking out, despite having gigs of free space. What a publicly embarassing way to learn out how useless that error message was.

That is all in preparation for my blinkenlights project. Seeing as how mythtv release 0.20 should be happening soon, it's a good time to check out the source and make the mods.
