2 Gigs!

On the train up to Toronto I checked out IntelliJ 6.0β and TeamCity, (as noted before). Well, IntelliJ was *terrible* - but it wasn't Jetbrains's fault. My MacBook only had 512MB of RAM in it, and desperately needed a brain augmentation.

Saturday I'd ordered some RAM from TigerDirect and Tuesday it arrived. The screwdriver required to upgrade memory is fairly small, so it took me some time to find the right screwdriver (i.e. cash outlay).

The first time after I had tried to install the memory, it didn't work. It takes a fair amount of pressure to get the memory properly seated, so much so that I had to brace against the outside of the laptop so that I was climber-style pinching (a large open hand grip) the laptop in order to get it in. Next, stuffing the EMI shielding back into place - since it expands inside of the RAM slot - was a bit difficult. Finally, 2 Glorious Gigs of RAM.

And it's fast. I've seen the busy-beach ball once since then, and I'm running Eclipse AND IntelliJ and Firefox and Mail and Adium and Quicksilver and an IRC client, etc, etc, etc, without any problems. Sweet.
