¿Qué Tal?

So here I am in Spain, with woefully inadequate Spanish. Nothing like necessity as motivation!

The people here are quite nice, and there are definitely aspects of European life I am happy to be back with like good food (succeeding at finding vegetarian resources so far, much to the chagrin of my meaty friends) and attitudes and so on. I've even got a line on a wall that is bouldered on frequently, and maybe a friend of a friend that climbs.

I should also note I find it ironic that I come to Europe to commute out to the suburbs - I thought that was a more North American thing, but the original contractee is resident in Castilla - la Mancha and no doubt is funded by the government, so the work location is Castilla - la Mancha, which is outside of Madrid.
