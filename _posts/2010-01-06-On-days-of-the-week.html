On days of the week

A Greek co-worker was telling me how the names of the week in Greek corresponded to the numerical order of the day in the week, rather than obscure references to Norse gods.

Sunday is onesday,
Monday is twosday,
Tuesday is threesday,
Wednesday is foursday,
Thursday is fivesday,
Friday is sixday,
Saturday is sevensday.

Saying this aloud amuses me. A lot.
