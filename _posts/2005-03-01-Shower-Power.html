Shower Power

Based on redflag's and Leif's recommendations, last night I picked up an Oxygenics Shower Head for $20 at Home Depot. It's a lo-flo shower head which aerates the water in a jet-like fashion. It kind feels like you're being hit by round drops instead of a stream, or you're standing in a stream of bubbles. Nicer than most lo-flos I've tried (mostly hostels).

Anywho, I'm excited to save the bunnies (not the killer ones, but the rest, y'know?) and save money on the electricity bill. What's really funny is I'm not sure if it is the 'aerated' action or maybe if I couldn't tell that the water was REALLY hot (which I kinda realized at first) but anyways, I was LOBSTER red after the first shower. The second shower resulted in non-strangeness.

I also installed an electronic thermostat - but I'm not convinced thats not going to cost more money. Heating the space in my apartment is a very weird thing - partially because of the high ceilings and the smoker who opens the windows and so on - so I think we used to always leave the heat off... And now it turns itself on! I'll have to keep turning it down until its unbearable.
