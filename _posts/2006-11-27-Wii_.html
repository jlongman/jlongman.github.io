Wii!

Truly an embarassment of riches...  Too many games, too little time.

Setup was easy, except for eveything being way over wrapped.  Stupid little green tape was everywhere.

Joan scoured Montreal Sunday after I got up early Saturday and roused her to check out the Zellers. I saw tons of accessories, but none to be found today.
The wii-mote is pretty nifty.  I can see already (after about 20 seconds) why people would have sore arms.  The wii-mote is hefty, and it vibrates, as feedback, as your pointer passes over things on the screen.  Pretty impressive.
ps> I finished Tony Hawk Project 8 at amateur level sometime late last night.
