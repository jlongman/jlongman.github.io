Missed a great show!

Crap! Crap! Double Crap!

Slashdot is irrelevant for any kind of 'news for nerds' that is timely. Case in point, a huge Coronal Mass Ejection (Good news, everyone lived! Well, except those that died) that hit the earth this weekend, resulting in incredible aurorae borealis... and I don't find out about it until this morning.

I think I'm going to pay attention to SpaceWeather now, although the SpaceWeatherPhone at min $US5/month, seems a bit excessive. There must be a free service that emails/sends SMS messages.
