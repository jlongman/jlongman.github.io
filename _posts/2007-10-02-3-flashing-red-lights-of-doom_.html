3 flashing red lights of doom!

Finally... I eluded it so long I thought it was merely for lesser beings...
Someone play Taps... I had just made Sergeant, Grade 3. I was going to push through to Gunnery Sergeant tonight, too.

The 360's three red rings of death.

Joan had picked it up for me as a Christmas present: just after release, and was only lucky enough to get this hot ticket item thanks to a snow storm. It served me well through Burnout (99% complete), Tony Hawk's American Wasteland, Halo 1 & 2, Colin McRae, Tony Hawk Project Eight, lots of Xbox Arcade games, and recently, Guitar Hero 2 (x2, since you can't buy a second guitar without the game!) and the kiss of death (apparently) Halo 3.

First they thought it might have been the power supply. If only. Then they said 3-4 weeks. The next call (5 minutes later) where I validated it wasn't the power supply, it was 4-5 weeks. Sigh.

Update: Just to make things interesting, it's intermittent. If it doesn't give the rings, it will lock up in Halo 3. Which isn't covered by the extended 3-year warranty. So now we have to hope the techs wherever are honest enough to try to find the 3-rings problem or I'll be out $110. (US or CAN, who cares! )

Right now GH2 is working, I wonder if Halo 3 would boot?

That way lies madness.

Second Update: Now it works!  Jeebus!  Yay Halo 3!  Didn't want to ruin my ranking by disconnecting in Ranked games so I just played Rumble Pit for a while, not enough to rank up.
