Sk8 or die!

I was checking out reddit when I came across a link, 'The Quarterlife Skateboard Crisis', it struck a chord and I middle-clicked it.

When I got around to reading it, it was right on the button. I'd had the same thoughts, every time I'd played Tony Hawk, when I added the eXtreme channel to the lineup, when I checked out the book of cool and when I located the various skate facilities in montreal.

Incidentally, I still haven't seen 'Lords of Dogtown', although I've had 'Dogtown and Z-boys' forever.

Of course, the other thought is to pick up some new aggressive inline skates, since I did do that for a while. And it was fun as far as I took it. But then I felt old at the age of 23 when I was doing that.
