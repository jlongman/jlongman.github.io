Investments

Picked up some anti-gravity gear: a new harness, since my old is getting stiff and unhappy-like, and I'm hoping to get some more lead climbing into my program; some Flashed climbing chalk - the chalk I'd been trying to track down since re-starting - this stuffs gunks itself onto your hands, 'so you don't have to'; an 'M-16″ brush — apparently the same design as that used to clean the rifle; some cheap athletic tape to help the tendons stay in place, even if it is placebo (can't find the reference online, anywho)...

Plus some non-anti-grav equipment: Graham twigged me onto the liquidation of certain sleeping bags at the MEC, so I picked up a Synthetic -7 Winterhawk (i think) mummy bag - for slightly over 50 bucks - to replace the TNF Cat's Meow, which isn't so meow since compressing with age (brrrr!); A down vest to keep me warm, since someone stole my last one while at Pastamore; a coupla shirts that Joan likes on me.

I'm pretty happy about being able to justify spending money on these items again. (Not that I necessarily didn't spend money, I just couldn't justify it ) Of course, I'll spend almost any amount of money for things which will make you happier and healthier. It's just hard to figure out what will really do that.
