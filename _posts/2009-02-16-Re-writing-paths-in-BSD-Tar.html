Re-writing paths in BSD Tar

Strip the beginning off:


tar -c -f foo.tar -C rootNboot -s,bootstrap/(.\*),\1, bootstrap

Use something innocuous as your regex character. Slash and hash are not good choices. I've also glossed over the details about escaping magic characters.

here's what python wants me to use


'tar -c -f foo.tar -C rootNboot -s' "\',^bootstrap/\(.\\\\*\\),\\1,\'" 'bootstrap'
