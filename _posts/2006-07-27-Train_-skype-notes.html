Train, skype notes

Funny, the Echo service on Skype seemed to work quite well, if a bit quiet due to ambient noise on my bluetooth headset. But apparently a message I left on Joan's answering machine was, I'm told, quite garbled. Maybe skype-out is fragile?

Charlie was pointing out that GPRS reorders packets more readily than satellite and since the Via/Opti Wifi is a hybrid network, it could mean that we were using a GPRS segment rather than satellite. Given a mean ping around 500ms but a peak as high as 970ms, perhaps there simply wasn't enough buffering for the audio.
