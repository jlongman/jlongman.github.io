Tony Hawk's UnderGround 2

A couple of months ago I picked up THUG2 on eBay for about $20 off retail. I've played (and finished) every Tony Hawk game except THUG (1) and handheld versions, but still covering DreamCast, Playstation, Playstation 2 and GameCube.

Anyways, this isn't really intended as a review, but rather a statement of progress. At the normal level I've completed Story and Classic mode, I'm a couple of goals away from completing insane classic mode (which is Insane), and every now and then I switch back to the story mode at the insane level.

I think it's important to have a record of where my time is wasted. So when I'm at the gates, defending my life, I can say, 'I finished THUG2 at insane level!', and Saint Peter will go, 'NO way, dude!'. Like that.
