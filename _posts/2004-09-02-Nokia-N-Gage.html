Nokia N-Gage

What a deal! For $CDN250 you get an unlocked, international phone, with Bluetooth, a colour screen, oh and plays games, oh and plays .WAV as a ringer! Oh, and it runs Java Midlets...

The N-Gage. With no sidetalkin'!. And I can switch back to my small phone thanks to SIM cards.

I'm convinced they are selling this as a loss-leader to their games, which is kind of silly considering that I downloaded Doom for it for free last night. Plus the Midlets you can get. One of the criticisms as a gaming platform is that the screen is portrait, not landscape. Frankly the phone is extremely useful without buying any games. (if a bit large, although maybe I'm biased). I've wanted to write code for something I carry with me since I went to JavaOne uh, in 1999 when they handed out Palm V's with early J2ME implentations.

(Christ, I've been doing Java for over 5 years now) (Crap, 7).
