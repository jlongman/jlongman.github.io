iPod Shuffle reset utility

Good news!  I've had a brain-dead 1st gen iPod Shuffle for the last 9 or so months.  The last time it worked, I was in Alabama!  It could have been the sub-zero night that did it in, although I do remember it playing between 4am and 6am since it was too cold to sleep that night.

The symptoms were basically that it was unaddressable as a flash drive on a Mac nor a PC and the lights would flash green/orange.  I'd scanned the forums and the collective response at the time was, send it back to Apple at half the cost of a new one (now even more than the cost of a refurbished one).  I was disappointed at the time but I also thought if the battery would ever completely drain then maybe it would reset.  So I'd pick it up again every week or so and get the lights to flash.
Today I was scanning the Apple website for fun stuff and found the iPod Shuffle reset utility. Sure enough, download it, install it, run it, *bang* got my iPod shuffle back.
Which is nice, as I have a second-gen iPod Shuffle (which is nice ) but it isn't as versatile since it requires a dock.  So I consider them two different tools in the tool belt:  the first-gen is data-oriented and the second-gen is portability oriented.
